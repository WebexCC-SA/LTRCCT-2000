## Feature Description

Using of Topic Analytics is now easier than ever. Instead of manually running topic collections, you can simply enable Topic Analytics for all your queues or select specific ones during setup. Once enabled, the system automatically analyzes interactions and discovers relevant topics. These topics are automatically applied as labels to your completed interactions, giving you immediate access to actionable insights at scale. Best of all, Topic Analytics is continuously learning over time, so it adapts as customer needs change without extra steps or manual retraining required.
